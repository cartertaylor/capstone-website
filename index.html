Welcome to CareerNet



Project Intro

There is currently a disconnect between the deans of the colleges at NAU and the tracking of student career before and after graduation. Team CareerNet is creating a web application dashboard to bridge this disconnect and track the career growth of NAU students and alumini. Our goal is to build a tool that can be used by Deans, chairmen, and faculty to explore how degree programs pan out for students that attend our school. This will allow them to use the data to create actionable plans to continue to improve NAU's degree programs.
